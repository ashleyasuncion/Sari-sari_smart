How to Use

Recording Sales
Go to Sales and tap "Record Today's Sales". Enter your stock expenses (Gastos sa Paninda) and today's earnings (Kinikita Ngayon). The app will automatically show your gross profit, estimated net profit, and revolving fund.
Adding Stock
Go to Stocks and tap "Add Stock". Fill in the item name, quantity, cost per unit, and selling price. You can also set a markup percentage to auto-calculate the selling price. Tap an item to update its visual status (Plenty / Getting Low / Out of Stock).
Tracking Utang
When completing a specific sale, enter a customer name to automatically record it as debt. You can also manually add debts from the Utang screen. Tap a customer to view their balance and history, then tap "Record Payment" to log payments.
Daily Summary
Your daily summary is shown on the Home screen with 4 cards: Today's Earnings, Stock Alert, Outstanding Debts, and End-of-Day Closing. You can also use the End-of-Day Closing routine to wrap up your store each day.
